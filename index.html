About Me

After completing a degree in psychology and sociology at Brighton University and gaining experience working with children in various roles, I decided to pursue a career in teaching. I completed a Postgraduate Certificate in Education at the University of East London. After a couple of years in teaching, having realised that I had a particular aptitude for maths teaching, I completed a further Postgraduate Certificate in Maths Specialist Teaching. Over the years, maths has become a passion of mine and by far my favourite subject to teach.

The fourteen years of experience I have had in the education sector have provided me with a broad spectrum of skills to offer the tech world. Working for the past few years as an assistant headteacher, I have developed excellent communication, leadership, team-work and mentoring skills which I have found essential in my coding journey so far.

I have a wide range of passions and interests, but my ultimate goal is to one day help create software which will aid the environment and sustainability- something I have been championing for many years in my various roles within education.
